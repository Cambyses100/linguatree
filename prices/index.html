Prices

Flexible Pricing Options

All lessons are tailored to your learning needs and schedule, with both Brazilian Portuguese and English lessons available.

One-to-One Lessons

Lingua1
One-to-one lessons are ideal for personalized learning. Prices depend on factors such as lesson duration and frequency. Lessons can focus on general language skills, business language, or specific cultural insights.

Group Lessons

LinguaGroup
Learn with friends or colleagues! Group lessons are available for two or more students, with pricing depending on group size and lesson length. Perfect for students who prefer learning in a collaborative environment.

In-house Training

LinguaTraining
If you represent a company looking to offer language training for staff, I can organize flexible lessons to suit your schedule. In-house training can be designed for both individuals and groups, with customized content for industry-specific language where needed.

Lessons for Children

LinguaChild
I offer tailored lessons for children who are learning Brazilian Portuguese or English. Whether your child is maintaining fluency or just starting their language journey, these lessons can be customized to fit their needs. Group lessons or after-school clubs can also be arranged.

Remote Learning

LinguaRemote
All lessons are available remotely via video conferencing. Learn from anywhere in the world, whether you prefer one-to-one sessions or group classes.
