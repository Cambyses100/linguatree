Tailored Learning

Customized Brazilian Portuguese and English courses to meet your individual needs.

No matter what your goals are for learning Brazilian Portuguese or English, I am here to help. I use a range of textbooks, audio materials, and carefully selected teaching techniques to create a course tailored specifically to you. Together, we can ensure that you achieve your goals effectively and at your own pace.

As part of my approach, I also include insights into Brazilian culture, covering topics like food, festivals, regional differences, and social etiquette. For example, you'll learn tips on how to navigate Brazilian social interactions, and understand important cultural nuances to feel more connected when communicating.

All courses start with a free 20-30 minute assessment where we’ll discuss your interests, background, and goals. This will help us design a course that fits your unique needs.

Lessons can focus on a range of skills, from speaking and listening to reading and writing, or any combination of these.

Flexible Learning Options

In addition to tailored lessons, I offer both in-person and remote lessons. You can learn from anywhere, whether you're at home, at work, or on the go. For in-person lessons, we can arrange meetings in convenient locations that suit your schedule.
