Courses

No matter what reasons you have to be interested in learning Brazilian Portuguese, I am happy to help you. I work with a range of textbooks, audio materials, and carefully chosen teaching techniques to tailor a course that meets your individual needs and allows you to achieve your goals effectively.

As part of my training in Brazilian culture, I will explain Brazilian food, festivals, regional differences, and do's and don'ts in different social contexts. For example, if you park your car in the street and a young boy says he will look after it, what should you do? How easy is it to make friends in Brazil, and what about beach etiquette?

All my courses start with a free 20-30 minute assessment during which teacher and student discuss the student’s interests, background, and goals so we can decide together on the best route to achieve the best results.

Courses are flexible: students can choose to focus on speaking skills, or they might prefer working on their reading and writing, or a mix of all skills.

Location is also flexible: lessons can take place in students' offices, homes, or public places like cafes in London or in parts of Surrey.

All of these factors will determine the cost of each course. Check the prices page for more information.
